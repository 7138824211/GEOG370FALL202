My map depicts the total number of residence in each county of Missouri that hold a Bachelors degree or higher. The data contains poeple from the ages of 25 and above. The three counties with the lowest population of poeple holding bachelors degrees or above were Worth County: 259 poeple, Schuyler County: 342 poeple, and Scotland County: 374 people. The three counties with the highest population of people holding bachelors degrees or more were St.Louis County: 297,633 people, Jackson County: 144,784 people, and St.Charles County: 99,685 poeple.
